Housing Levy Explained in Simple Terms

Published March 2026

The housing levy is a government deduction introduced to fund affordable housing projects in Kenya.

What Is the Housing Levy?

Employees contribute 1.5% of their gross salary while employers match the contribution.

Example Calculation

If you earn KSh50,000 your housing levy deduction will be KSh750.

Why the Government Introduced It

The program aims to reduce the housing deficit and increase affordable housing supply.
