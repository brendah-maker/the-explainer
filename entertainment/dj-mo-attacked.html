DJ Mo Escapes Death After Panga Attack in Murang’a

Published March 13, 2026 • 4 min read

Popular gospel entertainer Samuel Muraya, better known as DJ Mo, is recovering after a harrowing encounter with armed criminals in Kenol, Murang’a County. The incident has left fans and the entertainment industry in shock.

The Attack

According to reports, the father of two was accosted by a gang armed with pangas (machetes). While details surrounding the motive remain limited, the deejay survived the violent confrontation. The experience has been described as a "close shave" with death.

Trend of Insecurity

This attack follows a worrying trend of insecurity targeting high-profile public figures in Kenya. Just weeks prior, gospel singer Jimmy Gait was victimized by thugs in Runda, suffering injuries during a robbery. DJ Mo had actually been among the first to highlight Gait’s ordeal on social media, making this subsequent attack on him even more chilling.

Investigation and Public Response

Police in Murang’a have reportedly launched an investigation to track down the assailants. Meanwhile, messages of support have flooded social media as the "Crossover 101" star begins his recovery. Fans have expressed relief that DJ Mo survived and are urging authorities to enhance security for entertainers.

Conclusion

The incident highlights ongoing security challenges faced by public figures in Kenya. It also demonstrates the power of social media in rallying support for victims and raising awareness about personal safety in the entertainment industry.
